Wireless and Invisible

LiFi takes advantage of lights and dispenses with the router, so it works without the need for cables. In addition, it can operate with infrared light, which is invisible to the human eye, or with visible LED light at very low intensity so as to avoid disturbance.

More Secure

Light does not pass through walls like radio waves do, and this prevents intruders from intercepting LiFi communications through a wireless network

More Accessible

Any light fitting can easily be converted into an internet connection point, as only a simple LiFi emitter needs to be fitted.

No Saturation

Internet connection via light could prevent the collapse of the radio spectrum which, according to LiFi's inventor Harald Haas, could take place by 2025.
